ELECTRICAL PROCESS

The Arduino board outputs 5V and 40mA per GPIO pin, which is insufficient for the Brewista's need of 12V and 1.4A. To address this, we need to use external components to boost the voltage and current.

A motor shield is used to increase the voltage and current supplied to the motors. By using MOSFETs or transistors along with motor drivers, the shield can provide 12V and 1.2A to power the 12V pumps in our project.
